Akan Naming Tradition

The Akan people of Ghana have a rich cultural tradition of naming children based on the day of the week on which they are born. These names, known as Akan names, are believed to influence a person’s character and identity. Each day has specific names for males and females, reflecting the deep connection between culture, time, and personal identity in Akan society.
